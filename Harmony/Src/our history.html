Our History

Since

1997


Since

1997 2001 2009 2020

1997, The Carpenter's Shop was established by a group of passionate craftsmen with a vision to create exceptional furniture pieces. Situated in a modest workshop, the early days were marked by dedication and perseverance despite initial challenges in establishing a foothold in the competitive furniture market. Driven by a commitment to craftsmanship and quality, The Carpenter's Shop gained local recognition for its attention to detail and personalized service. Each piece of furniture was crafted with precision, reflecting both the founders' skills and their deep passion for woodworking. Despite the economic uncertainties of the time, the team's unwavering dedication to quality soon garnered a small but loyal customer base.

The year 2001 marked a significant turning point as The Carpenter's Shop was chosen to manufacture furniture for apartment projects in Ho Chi Minh City. With the rapid development of apartment complexes, there was a growing demand for high-quality furniture. Recognizing this potential, we focused on manufacturing and supplying furniture for these projects. Being selected as a contractor required us to upgrade our production scale, invest in modern equipment, and train our workforce. This event elevated our reputation and status in the industry, and we developed new designs that aligned with contemporary trends and met customer needs.

In 2009, The Carpenter's Shop secured its first export orders to Europe, marking the beginning of a new chapter. Entering the international market was both a challenge and an opportunity to prove our product quality and enhance our brand's reputation. We upgraded our production lines and adopted international quality standards, investing in research and development to create products for European customers. These initial orders established our presence in the international market and opened up new business opportunities. Successful exports brought economic benefits and elevated our reputation,
motivating us to continue innovating.

In 2020, The Carpenter's Shop rebranded as Harmony Company, marking a significant strategic transformation. This change underscored our commitment to enhancing product quality and service to meet increasing customer demands. With the new name, Harmony Company positioned its products in the near-luxury segment, targeting customers seeking
superior quality furniture at reasonable prices.
We heavily invested in research and development, focusing on innovative designs that blend aesthetic appeal with practicality, meeting the high standards of the near-luxury market. The rebranding attracted many new customers while reinforcing trust among existing ones. Harmony products received acclaim for their design, quality, and attention to detail, solidifying our market position and confirming our reputation in the furniture industry. 2020 also marked a new phase in our global market expansion efforts. We worked diligently to bring Harmony products closer to international customers, creating numerous opportunities for collaboration and growth. With a clear vision and strategic direction, Harmony Company remains dedicated to delivering the best value to our customers and community. Our commitment to quality and innovation continues to drive our growth and success, ensuring we stay at the forefront of the furniture industry.
