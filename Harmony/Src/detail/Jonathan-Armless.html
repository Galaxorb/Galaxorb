Story


The sofa’s sweeping backrest and curved seat provides the perfect place to relax and unwind.

Designer Stine Aas has carefully considered every element of the sofa, combining organic shapes to create furniture that mirrors the shape of the human body. The curved shape and backrest combine to create a gentle and cosy space. The powder-coated steel legs are unobtrusive.


Designed by


Stine Aas

With an MA in spatial and furniture design from Bergen Academy of Art and Design, Stine Aas creates products and furniture that combine character and simplicity.

Her work is inspired by the everyday interactions between people and the objects around them, and she typically combines the visual, the tactile and the functional in her design. Working from Bergen, Aas has exhibited internationally in Stockholm, Helsinki, London, Milan, New York.
