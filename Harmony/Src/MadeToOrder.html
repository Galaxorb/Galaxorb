MADE TO ORDER

What does ‘made to order’ mean?

When an item is labeled as 'made to order', it means we do not currently have that item in stock in Vietnam.
The time period specified in the 'made to order' section is the estimated delivery time for the product. Estimated in weeks, this time period describes how long it will take the brand to produce your items plus the time needed to ship from the brand to Harmory.
This lead time is an estimate for your guidance and is subject to change. A more accurate delivery estimate will be provided to you via email within 14 days of your order.

Delivery from Harmor

Once delivered to us, your goods will be dispatched to you as quickly as possible. The shipping method and transit time applicable to your order will depend upon your location in the world, the delivery option chosen by you at checkout and the size and weight of the products purchased.
If you have any questions regarding delivery options, please see the FAQs on our delivery page or contact our team, who would be happy to help.
Please note - A small number of our brands participate in biannual seasonal shutdowns, which in some cases, can affect lead times. For more information or if you have any questions about these shutdowns, please see the FAQs on our Help and Advice page or contact our team who are happy to help.
